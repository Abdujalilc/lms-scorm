Other Important Initiatives

E.O. 13548 is not an isolated effort, but works in coordination with:

  • E.O. 13518—Employment of Veterans in the Federal Government, issued on November 9, 2009
  • Presidential Memorandum—The Presidential POWER Initiative: Protecting Our Workers and Ensuring Reemployment, issued on July 19, 2010
  • E.O. 13583—Establishing a Coordinated Government-wide Initiative to Promote Diversity and Inclusion in the Federal Workforce, issued on August 18, 2011

These initiatives and many others ensure the Federal Government can recruit, retain, and honor the best workforce for the American people.
