Resources for Recruiting People with Disabilities

There are a number of resources currently in existence that can help agencies make progress toward increasing employment of people with disabilities. Agencies should make full use of these resources to tap into the great potential of people with disabilities. Some of these Federal and community programs include:

  • Feds Hire Vets
  • Workforce Recruitment Program
  • Vocational Rehabilitation
  • The OPM Shared List of People with Disabilities
  • Ticket-To-Work Employment Networks
  • American Job Centers
  • Local Service Organizations and Other Resources
